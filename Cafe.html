Desserts

Desserts at our café embody elegance in every bite, blending refined flavors with captivating presentation. Crafted from premium ingredients like Valrhona chocolate and Madagascar vanilla, offerings such as classic tiramisu with layers of velvety mascarpone, rich espresso, and a dusting of fine cocoa deliver an exceptional experience. Each dessert is presented with an aesthetic that delights the eye, reflecting elegance and attention to detail, making it the perfect finale to your dining experience.

Tea

Our teas are a celebration of elegance, curated to elevate your senses with every sip. Sourced from the finest estates, each blend—whether the delicate floral notes of Darjeeling First Flush, the robust warmth of Assam Black, or the soothing herbaceous whispers of chamomile—offers a refined experience. Served in fine porcelain, each presenting a harmonious balance of aroma and flavor. Accompanied by a touch of honey or fresh lemon, every cup is a moment of sophistication, crafted to complement the serene ambiance of our café.
